Gameplay

Welcome to Castle Quest.

Are you more of a conqueror, than a traveler?
The Castle Quest is the right journey for you.

Find the hidden markers of all seven castles and scan them all to earn wonderful badges and become the True Conqueror of all Westeros!

After scanning the markers with your phone, move the circle cursor towards the button and click on it to collect your badge.

Would you also enjoy an extra adventure? And what about extra prizes? Check out Game of Sponsors , this game's main quest for the discovery of all the houses and knowledge available in the realm.

Disclaimer: The augmented reality features might have some issues. If that happens, try reloading the page or covering the camera with your hand for a few seconds, and position your phone parallel relatively to the marker
