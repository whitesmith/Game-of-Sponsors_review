Gameplay

Welcome to the Game of Sponsors.

By starting this journey, you have an opportunity to become the traveler with the most exquisite and rare badges.
Your main goal is to find all the augmented reality markers belonging to each of Pixels Camp's sponsors and scanning them here.

Once you scan a marker, you'll learn a bit more about each house (sponsor) and afterwards you'll be faced with a question about that house.

To answer the question, move the circle cursor towards the desired answer button and click on it to reveal your fate!

Well, if you want to find out more about it, why not play?
But remember, "When you play the Game of Sponsors, you win or you die".

Would you also enjoy an extra adventure? And what about extra prizes?
Check out Castle Quest, a side quest of Game of Sponsors to receive the Ultimate Badge!

Disclaimer: The augmented reality library used is still quite experimental. Only devices and OS's that support WebRTC will be able to use it.
Ideally use Chrome or Safari for a better experience.
If the scanning has any issues, try reloading the page or covering the camera with your hand for a few seconds, and position your phone parallel relatively to the marker.
